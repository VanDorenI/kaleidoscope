Kaleidoscope: more

A kaleidoscope creates an image that exists out of a continually changing pattern of shapes and colours. That is why I chose this as the name for my font. This font is created from a grid consisting out of rectangles and circles. From this grid three different weights were created. The font was made out of shapes that where placed on the grid. This has resulted in a colorful and playful font that seems to dance on the page.

    • Indra Van Doren
